Guides

Timeframes & Feedback Process:

1. The default feedback sending window is 7 days, and the received data retention period is 37 days.

2. Initializing a new feedback round generates a code, which can be shared with employees. They can use this code to send feedback through the employee portal.

3. Feedback sending windows cannot overlap. Once the feedback sending window for an active round is over, another feedback round can be initiated. This prevents the possibility of targeted code distribution.

4. If you require a longer data retention or feedback sending window, please refer to the pricing page.

5. Please note that the sending window cannot be less than 7 days. This duration ensures employees have sufficient time to formulate valuable feedback, potentially using their own devices to avoid possible device session playback and other tracking methods.

6. When employees send feedback, it is also routed to the oversight portal. This allows the oversight account holder to monitor and analyze raw feedback directly, increasing the likelihood of specific ideas reaching more stakeholders and generating higher quality feedback.

7. Feedback is encrypted. We do not have access to the content of the feedback sent.

8. Sent feedback does not display the author's account details

Oversight:

1. The email used for the oversight account must be different from that of the employer account.

2. The oversight email should generally belong to company stakeholders whose interest in employees' voices would encourage employees to generate specific, valuable, and constructive insights.

3. An oversight account can be linked to more than one employer account. However, feedback is organized first by linked employer accounts and then by active feedback rounds for those employers.

4. The data retention time for oversight accounts matches that of the linked employer accounts. However, terminating an employer account will not remove data from the oversight account before the end of the data retention period.
